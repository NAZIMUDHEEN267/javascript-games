how to play?

The game basically known as "rock paper scissor". There are three items are available

  • rock
  • paper
  • scissor

When you click the "play" button.

Computer shuffle the items and give you a result box. That will show each player items (highlighted in players colors)

Scores are added on scoreboard, which player first hit "10" points, that player will be the winner

game rule

  1. rock can beat the scissor
  2. scissor can cut the paper
  3. paper can wrinkle the rock

players

In this game there are two players, computer and You (when the game start, you can see a comment box that asking you to enter your name. That name will be show in the scoreboard and players box)
